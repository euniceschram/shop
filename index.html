How to Use

Lighter and Easier

This product weighs only 2.97 pounds, which is about 60% lighter than traditional garden hoses. It uses a special braided design with high flexibility, which can easily bypass narrow corners, trees and other obstacles, making watering work easy. The lightweight feature makes it easier for you to carry it to any outdoor scene you want to use, making your maintenance work easier.

No more Leakage

Aluminum-plated Copper Interface:Both ends of the garden hose are made of aluminum-plated copper metal, which has good pressure resistance and is not easy to damage. We configure three spare high-strength leak-proof washers (for the female end interface) for each product to ensure that the water hose will not leak. The connector design allows two water hoses to be connected together. With a leak-proof interface design, this hose can help you complete gardening work efficiently.

No-Kinks, Smoother Flow

This flexible garden hose is made of high-quality PVC pipe and high-density wear-resistant textile material. It will not kink, twist or burst during use. The unique PVC inner tube has high strength and pressure resistance, ensuring that the sturdy water hose can withstand greater water pressure without unexpected water hose bursting. If the hose is accidentally tangled, it is also easy to untie, saying goodbye to those traditional garden hose that are easy to kink.

Easy to Carry and Store

Due to the material characteristics, this garden hose is lighter, knot-free and kink-free, making it easy for you to carry, and easy to roll up and store when you want to. This kink-free garden hose comes in a storage box, which is perfect for optimizing storage space and keeping the hose tidy. The box allows you to easily place the hose without taking up too much space, while also protecting the hose from dust and dirt.

Multiple Scenarios, wide Range of Uses

This fabric hose is suitable for a variety of situations, including watering the garden, cleaning the house, car and yard, and bathing the pet. At the same time, the High-pressure garden hose has a burst water pressure of 550PSI, and can be used efficiently in most extreme weather conditions such as high temperature and freezing. It is important to have a hose that can adapt to a variety of environments and climates.
